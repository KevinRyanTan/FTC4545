HoloProtov5.c Documentation

Abilities of the software:

1. Controls a robot with 6 inch metal mecanum wheels
2. Operates a double motor lift, with 4 motors on the lift in total
3. Has automatic lift stabilization to hold it in place
4. Can operate rear servos to latch onto the goals
5. Has macros to go to any of the goal heights, interruptible at any time by the driver
6. Able to operate a motor-powered manipulator
7. Adjustible to any wiring setup

Limitations of the software:
1. NXT can only handle so many communications
2. Slight delay between actions
3. Currently 9 motors on the robot (Exceeding FTC limit)
