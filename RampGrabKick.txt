RampGrabKick.c Documentation

Steps:
1. Starts on the ramp (backwards)
2. Rolls backwards down the ramp
3. Grabs onto the 60 cm. goal while the robot is still rolling.
4. Stops moving
5. Move forwards
6. Turn right 30 degrees
7. Move forward
8. Turn 135 degrees right
9. Drop the balls into the goal
10. Moves the robot back a bit
11. Releases the goal
12. Moves forward to read the IR beacon
15a. In position 3, turn right 45 degrees. Then move forward a bit. Next, turn left 45 degrees
and run into the kickstand
15b. In position 2, move forward. Next, turn right 45 degrees, and finally run into the kickstand.
15c. In position 1, turn right 45 degrees. Then move forward and turn left 135 degrees.
Finally, run into the kickstand.

Capabilities:
1. Score up to 100 points in the autonomous period.
2. Has a 90%+ success rate
3. Precisely rotate using the Gyroscopic sensor
4. Determine the position of the center structure quick
5. Able to work well with many floor autonomous programs

Limitations:
1. Can have inaccuracies due to driving down the ramp, making the robot careen a bit.
2. Only allows for 100 points
3. In danger of being run into by the opposing alliance
